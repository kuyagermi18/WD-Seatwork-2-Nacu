Poseidon is the powerful god of the sea, earthquakes, storms, and horses. Known as the Earth-Shaker, he wields a massive trident capable of stirring oceans and splitting land. After helping defeat the Titans, he claimed the oceans as his realm. Poseidon is unpredictable and temperamental, reflecting the volatile nature of the sea. Yet he is also a creator — credited with forming horses and shaping islands. Worshiped by sailors and feared by coastal cities, Poseidon commands both awe and reverence. He is both protector and destroyer, embodying the beauty and danger of the ocean itself.
