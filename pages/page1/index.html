⚡ Zeus – King of Olympus

Zeus, the mightiest of the Olympians, is the god of the sky, lightning, thunder, law, and order. Born to the Titans Cronus and Rhea, he escaped being devoured by his father and later overthrew him, establishing the reign of the Olympian gods. As king of Mount Olympus, Zeus wields the thunderbolt — a powerful weapon forged by the Cyclopes — and uses it to maintain divine authority and cosmic balance. He is known for his commanding presence, sense of justice, and sometimes unpredictable temper. Zeus is also the father of many gods and heroes, symbolizing leadership, strength, and the complexities of power.
