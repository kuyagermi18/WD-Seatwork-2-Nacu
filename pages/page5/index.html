Apollo, the radiant son of Zeus and Leto, is the god of the sun, music, poetry, healing, prophecy, and archery. Twin brother to Artemis, he is a beacon of harmony, order, and light. Apollo drives his chariot across the sky each day, bringing light to the world. He is the patron of the Oracle of Delphi, where mortals sought divine insight. With his golden lyre, he inspires artists and poets; with his bow, he defends what is just.
