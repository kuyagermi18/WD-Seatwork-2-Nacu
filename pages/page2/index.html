Hades – King of the Underworld

Hades, the eldest son of Cronus and Rhea, rules the underworld — the shadowy realm of the dead. After drawing lots with his brothers Zeus and Poseidon, Hades claimed dominion over the afterlife. Often misunderstood as evil, Hades is instead stoic, fair, and devoted to the balance between life and death. He possesses a helm of invisibility and governs over wealth hidden beneath the earth, such as precious metals and gems. His presence is rarely seen above ground, but his influence is ever-present in the natural cycle of life and decay. He ensures that the souls of the dead receive the justice they deserve.
